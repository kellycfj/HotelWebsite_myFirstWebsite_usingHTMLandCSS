Our History


We are a Brazilian / Irish family based in Killarney since 2013. We have been welcoming new visitors and old friends ever since.

The Idea for starting a Guest House came from discussions with friends in 2016 about accommodadtion in Killarney. ...
We saw the need for a confortable place to relax with friends (and friends of friends!). A place that felt like a home away from home. All this within a short walking distance of our beloved National Park.

About the name, Acerola cherry is a tree that is native to tropical regions such as Brazil. Despite being one of our favourite fruits in Brazil, we have a cute Acerola tree in the garden of our home in Brazil. Naming our B&B as Acerola brought a nice feeling of connection.
